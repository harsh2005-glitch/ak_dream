About Us

Established in 2018, A.K. INFRADREAM LIMITED is a top real estate company dedicated to improving living standards. Based in Prayagraj, we’re on a mission to transform rural areas with beautiful residential and commercial plots in Uttar Pradesh, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh.

We're proud to serve as a major real estate company offering a wide range of plots to suit your needs. Whether you're looking for residential or commercial plots, A.K. Infra Dream has you covered.
